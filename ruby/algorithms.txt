Riding an elevator.  
1.  Receive input for which floor you want to enter.
2.  Start with the number one and keep adding one until that value is equal to the floor you want to enter.
3.  Once the two values are equal press the button of that value. 

Eating cereal

1. Pour milk in a bowl.
2. Put cereal in a bowl.
3. Put spoon in cereal.
4. Keep putting spoon in random places.
5. Eliminate each place spoon was placed.
6. Stop putting spoon somewhere once the mouth is reached.
7. Repeat until the spoon does not have any cereal.
